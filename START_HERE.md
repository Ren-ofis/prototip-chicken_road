## 🎯 The Game

**Chicken Crossing** is a crash-style betting game where you:

1. **Place a bet** ($10-$5,000)
2. **Guide a chicken** across an endless road
3. **Watch multiplier grow** with each lane crossed
4. **Press SPACEBAR** to cash out and keep winnings
5. **Get hit by traffic?** You lose the bet

**The catch:** Higher multipliers = riskier roads = more vehicles

## 🎮 How to Play

### Controls
- **Left Edge (30%)** = Move left
- **Center (40%)** = Move forward  
- **Right Edge (30%)** = Move right
- **SPACEBAR** = Cash out
- **Click buttons** = Menu actions

### Goal
Navigate the chicken across traffic lanes while the multiplier grows. Cash out anytime to secure your winnings. Get hit and lose everything!

### Modes
- **Manual**: You control timing
- **Auto**: Auto cash-out at target multiplier
- **Endless**: Free play, high score mode
